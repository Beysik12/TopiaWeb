Artificial intelligence (AI), which stands for systems or machines that resemble human intelligence to complete tasks and can iteratively improve themselves based on the information they acquire, is most commonly used to describe such systems or machines. I get the impression that many individuals are fascinated by the promise of AI. These breakthroughs imply some amazing things and potential benefits, but there are also possible undesirable consequences, as suggested by the cautionary title. We must consider our future plans as well as our needs and desires from technology. Technology is developing more quickly than ever before, and it is taking less time for it to become widely used. From writing to printing press, it took around 10,000 years, but just another 500 years for e-mail to be invented. It appears that a new era is just beginning. The era of AI A subfield of computer science called artificial intelligence (AI) studies how to simulate intelligent behavior in machines. AI is educating the machine, which makes it smarter.
